About -Us

Why Choose Us?

At Cruiseboxx, we understand that sometimes life can become overwhelming with stress creeping in on you. We also understand that most times, all you need are enjoyable moments from events you enjoy, that offer you a refreshing break from life's troubles.

Imagine being on a budget and there are no event-available-platforms that care enough to cater for your needs?

Well we at CruiseboXx are passionate about you and love to see hard workers, fun lovers, and driven individuals like you - regardless of your budget, location, and time- find true happiness and build the life you so earnestly desire.

MISSION

Our mission is to connect you to events happening around you to help you relax, network, and mingle, with an endless number of fun updates that suit your preferences. We are keen on self-care, and acknowledge that for each us to collectively build and sustain a functional society, we need you to be the best version of yourself.
